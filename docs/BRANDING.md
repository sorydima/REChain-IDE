# REChain IDE Brand Guidelines

This document provides guidelines for the use of the REChain Quantum-CrossAI IDE Engine brand assets and identity.

## Brand Overview

### Mission
To democratize access to quantum computing and advanced AI technologies by providing a unified, intuitive development environment that empowers developers to create innovative solutions for tomorrow's challenges.

### Vision
To become the world's leading development environment for quantum and AI-powered applications, enabling developers to seamlessly integrate cutting-edge technologies into their projects.

### Core Values
1. **Innovation**: Pushing the boundaries of what's possible
2. **Accessibility**: Making advanced technologies available to all
3. **Collaboration**: Fostering community and shared success
4. **Excellence**: Commitment to quality and best practices
5. **Integrity**: Ethical development and transparent practices

## Logo Usage

### Primary Logo
The primary logo consists of the REChain wordmark with the quantum-cross symbol. It should be used in most applications where the full brand identity is appropriate.

### Logo Variations
1. **Horizontal Lockup**: Wordmark to the right of the symbol (preferred for most uses)
2. **Vertical Lockup**: Wordmark below the symbol (for constrained spaces)
3. **Symbol Only**: The quantum-cross symbol without wordmark (for avatars, favicons, etc.)

### Clear Space
Maintain clear space around the logo equal to at least 25% of the logo height on all sides.

### Minimum Size
The logo should never appear smaller than 24px in digital applications or 0.5 inches in print.

### Color Usage
1. **Primary Color**: Quantum Blue (#2563EB)
2. **Secondary Color**: Quantum Purple (#7C3AED)
3. **Accent Color**: Quantum Green (#10B981)
4. **Neutral Colors**: White (#FFFFFF), Light Gray (#F3F4F6), Dark Gray (#1F2937)

### Backgrounds
- Use the full-color logo on light backgrounds
- Use the white logo on dark backgrounds
- Ensure sufficient contrast for accessibility

## Typography

### Primary Font
**Inter** is the primary font for all digital applications:
- Headlines: Inter Bold
- Body Text: Inter Regular
- Captions: Inter Light

### Secondary Font
**JetBrains Mono** is used for code examples and technical documentation.

### Font Usage Guidelines
- Maintain consistent font sizes and weights
- Use appropriate line spacing (1.5x font size)
- Ensure proper contrast ratios for accessibility

### Voice Characteristics

- **Approachable**: Friendly and inclusive
- **Forward-thinking**: Innovative and progressive
- **Reliable**: Trustworthy and consistent

### Tone Guidelines
1. **Technical Documentation**: Clear, precise, and detailed
2. **Marketing Materials**: Enthusiastic and visionary
3. **Community Communications**: Supportive and engaging
4. **Crisis Communications**: Calm and transparent

### Writing Principles
- Use active voice
- Be concise and direct
- Avoid jargon when possible
- Be inclusive in language
- Maintain consistency

## Do's and Don'ts

### Logo Usage
**DO:**
- Use approved logo files
- Maintain clear space
- Use appropriate colors for background
- Keep minimum size requirements

**DON'T:**
- Alter logo proportions
- Change colors or add effects
- Rotate or distort the logo
- Use low-resolution versions

### Color Usage
**DO:**
- Use primary palette consistently
- Maintain accessibility standards
- Apply colors purposefully
- Consider cultural associations

**DON'T:**
- Create unauthorized color variations
- Use colors inconsistently
- Ignore accessibility requirements
- Use too many colors together

### Typography
**DO:**
- Use approved font pairings
- Maintain consistent hierarchy
- Ensure proper spacing
- Consider readability

**DON'T:**
- Use unauthorized fonts
- Mix too many font variations
- Ignore line spacing
- Compromise readability
