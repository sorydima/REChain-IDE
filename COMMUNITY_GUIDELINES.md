# REChain Quantum-CrossAI IDE Engine Community Guidelines

## Welcome to Our Community

Welcome to the REChain Quantum-CrossAI IDE Engine community! We're excited to have you as part of our growing community of developers, researchers, and innovators working together to revolutionize software development through AI, quantum computing, and Web6 technologies.

## Our Mission

Our mission is to create an inclusive, collaborative, and innovative community that drives the development of the REChain IDE Engine while fostering learning, sharing, and growth among all participants.

## Community Values

### Inclusivity

We welcome contributors from all backgrounds, experiences, and identities. We believe that diverse perspectives lead to better solutions and innovation.

### Respect

We treat all community members with respect and courtesy, regardless of their experience level, background, or opinions. Disagreements are natural, but personal attacks are not acceptable.

### Collaboration

We believe in the power of collaboration and teamwork. We encourage knowledge sharing, peer review, and constructive feedback.

### Innovation

We foster an environment that encourages experimentation, creativity, and pushing the boundaries of what's possible in software development.

### Transparency

We maintain open communication about project decisions, progress, and challenges.

## Code of Conduct

All community members are expected to follow our [Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md), which includes:

- Being respectful and inclusive
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being mindful of your surroundings and fellow community members
- Respecting different viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community

## Ways to Participate

### Contributing Code

1. Fork the repository
2. Create a feature branch
3. Make your changes
4. Write tests for your changes
5. Update documentation as needed
6. Submit a pull request

### Reporting Issues

- Use the issue tracker for bug reports and feature requests
- Follow the issue templates
- Provide detailed information about the problem
- Be responsive to questions from maintainers

### Code Review

- Participate in code reviews for pull requests
- Provide constructive feedback
- Follow our code review guidelines
- Be respectful and professional

### Documentation

- Help improve documentation
- Translate documentation to other languages
- Create tutorials and examples
- Write blog posts about your experiences

### Community Support

- Answer questions on community forums
- Help newcomers get started
- Share your knowledge and experiences
- Participate in community discussions

## Community Roles

### Contributors

Anyone who contributes to the project through code, documentation, testing, or other means.

### Maintainers

Experienced contributors who help review and merge pull requests, manage issues, and guide the project's direction.

### Moderators

Community members who help moderate discussions, enforce the code of conduct, and maintain a positive environment.

### Ambassadors

Community members who actively promote the project, organize events, and help grow the community.

## Conflict Resolution

### Reporting Issues

- Report code of conduct violations to community@rechain.ai
- Provide detailed information about the incident
- Maintain confidentiality when requested

### Investigation Process

2. Investigate the incident
3. Take appropriate action
4. Follow up with the reporter

- Opportunity to appeal decisions
- Review by different community members
- Final decision by project maintainers
